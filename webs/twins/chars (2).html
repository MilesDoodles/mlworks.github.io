Characters

Berry Lollipop

Voiced by: Malory Griffyn
Birthday: November 22
Age: 21
Gender: Female
First appearance: Season 1, Episode 1: Berry the Fireman.

A very sweet girl (literally). Full of joy and innocense. She loves her sister with a strength nobody can describe. She's willing to face everything in order to keep her loved ones safe.

She's a playful little soul with the body of a grown ass woman. She has a lot of skills. She can be your fireman,
your playmate, your daughter, your wart, your teacher, your farmer, your gardener, your morally questionable
fantasy, etc.

Berry also has an alter ego that only shows up whenever you mess with her sister, Cherry.

Cherry Lollipop

Voiced by: Mel Ruiz
Age: 21
Birthday: November 21
Gender: Female
First appearance: Season 1, Episode 1: Berry the Fireman

An easily irritable individual, she loves to keep her house clean and shiny (something Berry doesn't really help with), she likes to do the groceries to refresh her state of mind and loves soap operas with all her heart.
She also loves to __________ every time she can, that's why she also loves to be alone.

Cherry is the kind of girl that won't show any sign of love towards her sister at all, yet would not hesitate to beat you up if you ever do anything to her. She has no particular addictions (as of today).

She loves to spend time with her friend Jane and overly hates Berry's friend Stella and even though she loves men, it's tough for her to find a boyfriend due to lack of social skills and what she's been through.

She thinks Jane is hot even though she doesn't really like women.

Jane Marshmallow

Voiced by: Guttergal
Birthday: July 12
Age: 24
Gender: Female
First appearance: Season 1, Episode 2: Alone Again (Unnaturally)

Mintville's librarian. She's a very shy woman primarily focused on her library, with a passion for reading that has simply no comparison.

She's Cherry's best friend, and probably will ever be just that. Her preference towards women is something she likes to keep for herself, but sometimes she just can't hide how much she loves Cherry even though nothing can be said from the other side. Jane is a very responsible woman and her discipline is something to envy.

Her life is literally just work, hang out with Cherry and go back to sleep, and apparently she loves it that way.

Stella Strawberry

Voiced by: ?
Age: 17
Birthday: March 13
Gender: Female
First appearance: Season 1, Episode 2: Alone Again (Unnaturally)

Berry's best friend is a minor. Yeah, shocker.
Stella is a very kind-hearted witch. She's just a beginner when it comes to mastering witchcraft, but she tries really hard to be helpful for all of her friends (and Cherry).

She has an "Express Wizardry" business right in her home even though she's not a wizard, but a noob-witch.

Stella usually gets to be the voice of reason for Berry, Cherry, Fummy and Jane, and is the only one who actually hangs out with Fummy without any struggle from the other side.

She has a very soft spot for Berry, and is willing to play with her as long as she's happy.
