About us

Miracle Light Works is an emerging group in the mutimedia entertainment
sector specialized in the creation and development of innovative content.

We focus on the experimentation between old and modern technologies
and methods for animation and other audiovisual productions, offering
entertainment content targeted to an audience of 16 years and up through
parodies, original animations, music, webcomics, games, software and more,
combining creativity and technology to create unique experiences that catch
the attention of a variety of audiences.

Naming

"Miracle Light" is more than just a cute little name.
It reflects the hope in our dreams and the fate we have on them.
Sure, we might be carefree and we could take some things lightly but even then,
we would like to bring bigger, better and more enjoyable projects for our public.
